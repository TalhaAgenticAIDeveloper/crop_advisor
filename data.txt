Kisan bhai, main ne aap ki zameen aur mausam ka jaiza lia hai.

Mere hisab se sab se behtar fasal jo aap laga sakte hain, wo hai Sorghum (jowar). Ye fasal taqreeban 120 din mein tayar ho jaye gi. Esko din mein ek baar pani dena hoga.

Dusri behtareen fasal aap ke ilaqay ke liye Palak hai. Ye lagbhag 45 din mein ready ho jati hai. Isko roz do dafa pani dena chahiye.

Teesri fasal jo aap ke liye faydamand ho sakti hai wo hai Gajar. Gajar 75 din mein ready ho jati hai. Esko sirf ek martaba pani dena kaafi ho ga.

In teenon mein se aap kisi bhi fasal ka intekhab kar sakte hain. InshaAllah achi paidawaar aur faida hoga.
